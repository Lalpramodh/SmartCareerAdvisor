About Our Project

Our Career Recommendation System is designed to help users find suitable job roles based on their skills, interests, and academic details.

Key Features

  • Smart career recommendations
  • Detailed job role suggestions
  • Roadmaps & skill guidance
  • Simple, user-friendly interface

How It Works

  • User inputs academic & skill details.
  • System analyzes data using Flask & AI models.
  • Displays most suitable job roles.
  • Users explore & take career actions.
